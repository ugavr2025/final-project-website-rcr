Project Overview

Our project is a Virtual Sculpture / 3D Painting Studio built for VR. In this shared space, users can collaboratively ‚Äúsculpt‚Äù by placing and resizing primitive 3D objects like cubes and spheres, then recolor them to build complex scenes together. An AI-powered artist NPC observes the scene and delivers simple, context-aware feedback and reactions to keep the creative process fun and engaging.

The experience focuses on a small, manageable set of interactions: selecting shapes, moving and scaling them, changing colors, and undoing actions. Keeping sculpting operations relatively simple lets us spend more time polishing collaboration, feedback, and overall user experience.

Requirements Coverage

  • Collaborative: Multiple users can join the same scene and build sculptures together.
  • AI NPC: A floating artist character gives simple feedback lines based on what users build.
  • UI: In-VR toolbar for color selection, shape selection, and undo/redo actions.
  • Avatars: Users are represented via the default XR rig with tracked hands/controllers.
  • Anchors: Not required, since the app runs purely in VR (no mixed reality anchoring needed).
